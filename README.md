# AI Policy & Ethics Research

A collection of collaborative research papers examining responsible AI 
deployment, governance frameworks, and ethical evaluation methodologies.

## Reports

### AI Governance Policy Analysis
Comparative analysis of three AI governance frameworks — Anthropic's 
Responsible Scaling Policy, Ohio State University's AI Use Guidance, 
and Texas HB 149 — evaluating enforceability, transparency, and 
stakeholder accountability across corporate, institutional, and 
statutory governance tiers.

**Co-authors:** [Kamora McCowan, Jaiden Washington, Zoey Hall, Thane Douglass]

📄 [View Report](reports/ai-governance-policy-analysis.pdf)

---

### Ethical & Responsible AI Tool Audit
Structured ethical audit of Claude (Sonnet 4.6) and ChatGPT (GPT-4o) 
for clinical decision support, applying a four-category evaluation 
framework (AF1–AF4) across 8 criteria including bias detection, 
sensitive data handling, HIPAA awareness, and misuse resistance.

**Co-authors:** [Kamora McCowan, Jaiden Washington, Zoey Hall]

📄 [View Report](reports/ai-ethics-healthcare-audit.pdf)

## Topics Covered
- AI governance & policy frameworks
- Responsible AI standards (Anthropic RSP, EU AI Act, IBM AI Fairness 360)
- Ethical AI evaluation methodology
- Bias detection in clinical AI systems
- HIPAA compliance & sensitive data handling
